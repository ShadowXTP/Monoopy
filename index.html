How to Win

To win a game of Monoopy, a player must obtain a set amount of Victory Points (VP). The number of victory points is determined by the amount of players

3 Players:

Play to 21 Points

4 Players:

Play to 18 Points

5 Players:

Play to 15 Points

A Victory Point is earned when any of the following events/conditions occur:

  • Owning any property, station, or utility grants +1 point.
  • Having 1-4 houses on a property adds +1 point.
  • Acquiring an Achievement adds +1 Point

Trait Selection: Players roll dice; highest roller picks their first trait first. The order then continues with the player on the right of the first player. The last player picks two traits starting the second trait selection, and it continues left from the last player. Building Houses: You may only place one house per property per turn at the original price. Additional houses in the same turn cost double. If a house is sold, only 50% of the original cost is refunded. Trading: Limited to one trade per player per turn. Includes property, money, traits, and dev cards. Rent payments are excluded but can involve rent mitigation trades. Property Management: Total of 12 hotels and 40 houses are available. Eliminated players return all properties to the bank, excluding achievements. The Bank will pay $200 to the player who is owed money, if the eliminated player cannot. Miscellaneous: The Robber can be removed for a flat fee of $250 Rent cannot be collected while in jail
