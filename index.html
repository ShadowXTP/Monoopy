Main Objectives

To Win a game of custom monopoly, a player must obtain a set amount of Victory Points (VP). The number of victory points is determined by the amount of players;

  1. 3 Players: 21 points
  2. 4 Players: 18 points
  3. 5 Players: 15 points
A Victory Point is earned when any of the following events/conditions occur. Owning any property, station, or utility grants +1 point. Having 1-4 houses on a property adds +1 point. Owning a hotel provides an additional +1 point Acquiring an Achievement adds +1 Point Chance/Development Cards +1 Point
